CUSTOMER SEGMENTATION

Customer segmentation divides customers into groups based on purchasing behavior, spending patterns and tenure.

Premium Customers:
Customers with high spending and long-term engagement.

Regular Customers:
Customers with moderate spending and frequent purchases.

Occasional Customers:
Customers who purchase only during offers or seasonal periods.

Business Recommendation:
Premium customers should receive loyalty rewards and exclusive benefits.
Regular customers should receive personalized product recommendations.
Occasional customers should receive promotional discounts.

------------------------------------------------------------

CUSTOMER CHURN PREDICTION

Customer churn refers to customers who stop purchasing or discontinue services.

Important churn indicators:

Month-to-month contract

Low tenure

High monthly charges

Lack of technical support

No online security

Retention Strategies:

Offer annual subscription discounts.

Improve customer support quality.

Provide loyalty rewards.

Personalize offers based on purchase history.

------------------------------------------------------------

PURCHASE PREDICTION (LSTM)

Purchase sequence prediction identifies the next likely purchase based on previous buying behavior.

Benefits:

Increase repeat purchases.

Improve recommendation systems.

Optimize inventory management.

Business Insight:

Customers often purchase related products together.

Frequently purchased items should be recommended automatically.

------------------------------------------------------------

CUSTOMER SENTIMENT ANALYSIS

Sentiment analysis classifies customer reviews into:

Positive

Neutral

Negative

Positive reviews indicate customer satisfaction.

Negative reviews indicate quality issues, delivery problems or poor customer service.

Business Recommendation:

Monitor negative reviews frequently.

Respond quickly to customer complaints.

Improve products receiving repeated negative feedback.

------------------------------------------------------------

CUSTOMER RETENTION STRATEGIES

Improve after-sales support.

Provide personalized discounts.

Reward loyal customers.

Offer referral incentives.

Reduce customer complaints through faster issue resolution.

Collect customer feedback regularly.

------------------------------------------------------------

BUSINESS INSIGHTS

Positive customer sentiment generally increases customer retention.

Premium customers contribute the highest revenue.

Customers with technical support show lower churn probability.

Early identification of dissatisfied customers helps prevent churn.

Combining customer segmentation, churn prediction, purchase prediction and sentiment analysis enables intelligent customer relationship management.
